About Me

    Currently I am a Ph.D. candidate in State Key Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing (LIESMARS) at Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Gui-Song Xia and Prof. Liangpei Zhang. My major is remote sensing, and my research interests include land-cover classification, transfer learning, change detection, content-based retrieval, ect.
